Group Travel Tips

📱

Set Up a Group Chat

Create a WhatsApp or group chat for all 8 families before departure. Share real-time locations, rest stop alerts, and coordinate convoy driving across the island.

⛽

Fuel Up Often in NL

Gas stations are sparse in rural Newfoundland. Never let the tank drop below half. Twillingate and the stretch before it is particularly remote — fill up in Grand Falls.

📅

Book Everything in Advance

With 29 people you MUST pre-book: Western Brook Pond tour, Iceberg Quest, all restaurants, and ferry cabins. St. John's on Canada Day sells out months ahead.

🦟

Bug Spray is Essential

Late June in Newfoundland means blackflies and mosquitoes — especially in Gros Morne. Pack strong DEET spray for every family and apply before every outdoor activity.

🌧️

Embrace the Weather

Newfoundland can have sun, fog, rain, and wind all in one day — especially in St. John's. Layers and waterproofs are not optional. The best memories often come from the wet days!

🎟️

Canada Strong Pass = Big Savings

Free park entry is active Jun 19–Sep 7, 2026 covering Gros Morne, Signal Hill & Cape Spear. Download the Parks Canada app. Youth under 17 are always free year-round.

📶

Cell Coverage is Limited

Rural NL has spotty signal. Download offline Google Maps for all routes before you leave. Save important addresses (accommodations, ferries, restaurants) offline.

🐋

Wildlife Rules

Moose are a serious driving hazard in Newfoundland — especially at dawn and dusk. Drive cautiously on rural roads and watch for moose crossing signs. Also watch for bald eagles and puffins!

🍁

Canada Day — July 1st!

You'll be in St. John's for Canada Day — one of the best places in the country to celebrate. Free concerts, fireworks, street parties and flag-waving. Dress in red and white!
